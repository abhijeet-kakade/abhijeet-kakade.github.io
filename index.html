Who We Are

The Computer Student Association (COSA) is a dynamic organization dedicated to promoting technological education, innovation, and networking opportunities for computer science students.

Founded with the vision to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, COSA organizes various events, workshops, and competitions throughout the academic year to enhance the technical and soft skills of its members.

Our team consists of passionate students who are committed to creating a collaborative environment where members can learn, grow, and excel in their areas of interest.
